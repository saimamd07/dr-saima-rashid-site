About Dr. Saima Rashid

Dr. Rashid is a dedicated internist who values compassion, integrity, and personalized medicine, with a focus on giving patients more time, deeper support, and stronger doctor-patient relationships.

Dr. Rashid believes medicine is most effective when patients feel heard, supported, and empowered. Her approach emphasizes preventive care, timely access, and meaningful relationships that help patients pursue long-term health goals with clarity and confidence.
